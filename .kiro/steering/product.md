# Product Overview

## Harding Secondary School Website

A modern, professional website for Harding Secondary School in KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa. The site showcases the school's 70+ year legacy of educational excellence and serves as the primary digital presence for prospective students, parents, and the community.

## Purpose

- Present school information, programs, and achievements to prospective families
- Provide easy access to admissions information and application processes
- Showcase academic excellence (95% matric pass rate, 67% bachelor's pass)
- Highlight extracurricular activities including sports, arts, and leadership programs
- Share news, events, and announcements with the school community
- Facilitate contact and communication with school administration

## Target Audience

- Prospective students and their parents/guardians
- Current students and families
- Alumni and community members
- Educational stakeholders and partners

## Key Features

- Responsive design for all devices (mobile-first approach)
- Hero section with image slideshow showcasing school life
- News and events carousel
- Photo gallery with category filtering
- Contact forms for inquiries and applications
- Social media integration
- Professional navigation with dropdown menus
- Achievement statistics and highlights
